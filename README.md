# 🪑 Furniture Assembly Digital App
Built for Sarprofil — a leading Turkish furniture manufacturer

### Overview

The Furniture Assembly Digital App is a bilingual, AI-powered platform designed to modernize and simplify the furniture assembly experience. Instead of static paper manuals, users receive interactive 3D, step-by-step instructions tailored to their specific product.

The app reduces assembly errors, lowers customer support costs, and creates new post-purchase engagement opportunities for manufacturers.

Key Features
## 🔍 Product-Based Access

Users enter a product string or scan a QR code to instantly retrieve the correct assembly guide

## 🧩 Interactive 3D Assembly Guides

Real-time 3D visualizations for each assembly step

Clear sequencing of parts, tools, and actions

Designed for mobile and tablet use during assembly

## 🌍 Bilingual Support

Fully bilingual interface (e.g. Turkish / English)

Localized terminology for parts, tools, and instructions

## 🤖 AI-Powered Assistant

Integrated AI chatbot trained on the full product catalog

Reduces reliance on human customer support

## 🛒 Smart Upsell & Support Integration

Context-aware recommendations for:

Replacement parts

Compatible accessories

Similar or upgraded furniture models

Seamlessly embedded into the assembly flow
